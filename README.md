# 🎯 BATNA Strategy Planner Pro  

**The most beautiful & complete interactive BATNA tool on the internet**  
*Harvard Principled Negotiation Framework in a single HTML file*  

---

## ✨ Features  

- **Analyzer** → Scenario checklist + live Power Dynamics Radar Chart  
- **Build BATNA** → Harvard 3-step builder (List → Rate → Identify)  
- **ZOPA Visualizer** → Interactive sliders to explore the Zone of Possible Agreement  
- **Concepts** → In-depth explanations of BATNA, WATNA, Probabilistic BATNA, etc.  
- **Playbook** → Checklists, power phrases, phrases to avoid, and body language signals  
- **Mobile-first design** with bottom navigation (super smooth on mobile)  
- **100% offline** — no login required, no backend  

---

## 🚀 How to Use  

### 1. Scenario Analyzer  
Check all situations that apply to your negotiation. The tool will:  
- Provide strategic recommendations  
- Update the **Power Dynamics Radar Chart** in real-time  

**Example:**  
- **Situation:** The other party has many alternatives → stronger BATNA  
- **Strategy:** Focus on uniqueness & creative flexibility  

**Execution:**  
1. Identify gaps their alternatives don’t cover  
2. Become the risk-free option (trial, pilot, guarantee)  
3. Bundle creatively (don’t just cut price)  
4. Compete where you win (not price)  

---

### 2. Build Your BATNA (Harvard 3-Step Process)  

1. **List Alternatives** → What will you do if negotiation fails?  
2. **Rate Each Option** (1–10)  
3. **Identify Your BATNA** → The strongest option  

> ⚠️ Most people *overestimate* their BATNA. Be brutally honest.  

---

### 3. ZOPA Visualizer  

Use sliders to test if a deal is possible:  

- 🟢 Green → Agreement possible  
- 🔴 Red → No overlap  

---

### 4. Playbook  

- ✅ Pre-negotiation checklist  
- 💬 Power questions  
- ❌ Phrases to avoid  
- 👀 Body language signals
